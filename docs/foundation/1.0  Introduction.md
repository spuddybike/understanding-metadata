# Introduction to the Foundation training course

This module builds on the ideas introduced in the Introduction to Metadata course and prepares you to begin creating your own metadata. 
Here, we focus on understanding the practical decisions involved in documenting research data, what needs to be recorded, why it matters, and how metadata supports long‑term reuse, 
transparency, and research quality. The units in this section help you transition from understanding metadata conceptually to seeing how it applies directly to your own project.
